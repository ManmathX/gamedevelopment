About Our Club

Founded in 2020, our club brings together retro game enthusiasts from all walks of life. We celebrate classic game design, pixel art, and the spirit of vintage gaming.

What We Do:

  • Retro game jams & 8‑bit challenges
  • Workshops on pixel art & classic game design
  • Mentorship programs for budding retro developers
  • Exhibitions and showcase events
